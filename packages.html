Highland Trek Quest

Experience Sri Lanka’s highlands, tea trails, and rainforests on this adventure-packed tour. Hike scenic trails, visit waterfalls, explore wildlife parks, and discover cultural landmarks — perfect for nature and trekking enthusiasts.

🧭 Tour Overview


Meals: According to your preferences
Hotels: 09 nights of hotel accommodation
Transport: By air-conditioned vehicle

Book Now
Package Includes and Destinations
  • 🛕 Temple of the Tooth Relic – Kandy
    Visit Sri Lanka’s most sacred Buddhist temple, housing the revered Tooth Relic of the Buddha. Experience spiritual rituals and the cultural heart of the hill capital.
  • 🌄 Knuckles Trek (Part)
    Explore the foothills of the Knuckles Mountain Range with scenic forest trails. Enjoy fresh mountain air, biodiversity, and panoramic views.
  • 🌳 Udawatta Kele Sanctuary
    Walk through a protected forest reserve rich in birdlife and native flora. A peaceful nature escape in the heart of Kandy.
  • 🍃 Tea Factory & Plantation Visit
    Learn how world-famous Ceylon tea is produced from leaf to cup. Stroll through lush tea estates and enjoy fresh tea tasting.
  • 🏞️ Horton Plains National Park
    Hike through montane grasslands and cloud forests. Visit World’s End cliff and Baker’s Falls.
  • 🚶 Pekoe Trail – Stage 22 (Kandapola to Pedro Estate)
    Walk through scenic tea plantations and rural villages. One of the most beautiful stages near Nuwara Eliya.
  • 💦 Upper Diyaluma Waterfalls Trek
    Trek to Sri Lanka’s second-highest waterfall with natural rock pools. Enjoy breathtaking viewpoints and refreshing swims.
  • 🌉 Nine Arches Bridge
    Visit the iconic colonial-era railway bridge surrounded by jungle. Perfect spot for photos and train sightings.
  • 🏔️ Little Adam’s Peak
    An easy hike offering stunning views of Ella Gap and tea estates. Ideal for sunrise or sunset hikes.
  • 🐘 Udawalawa National Park Jeep Safari
    Experience a wildlife safari famous for wild elephants. Chance to see buffalo, deer, crocodiles, and birdlife.
  • 🌴 Sinharaja Rain Forest (Part)
    Guided walk in Sri Lanka’s last primary rainforest. Discover endemic plants, birds, and tropical ecosystems.
  • 🏙️ Colombo City Tour
    Explore key highlights of Sri Lanka’s commercial capital. A mix of colonial landmarks, modern life, and local culture.

Nature & Ruins Expedition

Explore Sri Lanka’s ancient cities, wildlife, waterfalls, and beaches on this culture-and-nature-packed tour. Discover UNESCO heritage sites, enjoy jeep safaris, trek scenic spots, and relax along pristine beaches

🧭 Tour Overview


Meals: According to your preferences
Hotels: 11 nights of hotel accommodation
Transport: By air-conditioned vehicle

Starting from: LKR

🥾 Activities Included
  • 🛕 Temple of the Tooth Relic – Kandy
    Visit Sri Lanka’s most sacred Buddhist temple, housing the revered Tooth Relic of the Buddha. Experience spiritual rituals and the cultural heart of the hill capital.
  • 🐘 Wilpattu National Park Jeep Safari
    Morning and/or evening safari to spot elephants, leopards, sloth bears, and abundant birdlife in Sri Lanka’s largest national park.
  • 🏛️ Anuradhapura Ancient City
    Explore the sacred city of Anuradhapura with its stupas, Bodhi tree, and ancient ruins. Learn about Sri Lanka’s early civilization and Buddhist heritage.
  • 🪷 Aukana Buddha Statue
    See the towering 12-meter high rock-carved Buddha statue, one of Sri Lanka’s finest ancient sculptures.
  • 🚤 Rural Village & Catamaran Boat Ride
    Experience rural life on a boat ride through peaceful waterways, observing local culture and scenic nature.
  • 🏰 Sigiriya Rock Fortress
    Climb the iconic UNESCO World Heritage site, with frescoes, water gardens, and panoramic views.
  • 🏛️ Polonnaruwa Ancient City
    Discover another UNESCO site, with well-preserved ruins, temples, and statues from Sri Lanka’s medieval kingdom.
  • 🛖 Barth in a Tank Experience
    Interact with locals and enjoy a unique traditional water activity in rural surroundings.
  • 🛕 Dambulla Cave Temple
    Explore the impressive rock temple complex with centuries-old Buddhist murals and statues.
  • 🌿Matale Spice Garden
    Learn about Sri Lanka’s spices, herbs, and medicinal plants. Enjoy hands-on demonstrations and aroma trails.
  • 💃 Kandy Cultural Dance Show
    Enjoy a lively traditional performance showcasing Kandyan dances, drumming, and folklore.
  • ⛪ Three Loops’ Temples – Kandy
    Visit a cluster of historic temples offering spiritual insight and scenic surroundings.
  • 🌄 Ramboda Falls & Tea Factory Visit – Nuwara Eliya
    Stop for photos at cascading waterfalls and tour a working tea plantation. Enjoy fresh Ceylon tea and high tea at the iconic Grand Hotel.
  • 🏞️ Kithulgala White Water Rafting
    Adventure through rapids surrounded by jungle scenery on a guided rafting experience.
  • 🏖️ Bentota Beach
    Relax on golden sands, enjoy water sports, or explore coastal nature along the tranquil southwest coast.
