About New Saniro Travels

Trusted Travel Solutions Backed by a Powerful Sri Lankan Business Legacy

New Saniro Travels is a trusted travel agency dedicated to providing reliable and affordable travel solutions for local and international travelers. Our goal is to make every journey smooth, comfortable, and memorable.

With strong industry connections and customer-focused service, we specialize in tour planning, hotel reservations, and airport transfer services. We prioritize safety, transparency, and customer satisfaction in every booking.

Whether you are traveling for leisure, business, or family vacations, New Saniro Travels is here to support you every step of the way.

New Saniro Group of Companies

The origins of the New Saniro Group of Companies date back to 1975, when Mr. Reginald Stanley and Mrs. Sirima Gamage founded the “Saniro” business. With family dedication and entrepreneurial vision, this small enterprise became the foundation of today’s New Saniro Industries.

In 1998, Mr. A.A.D. Imal Shantha Kumara and General Manager Mrs. T.D.M. Niluka Damayanthi established New Saniro Industries in Avissawella with only six employees. Today, it has grown into a large business group operating factories, showrooms, and hotels across Sri Lanka.
